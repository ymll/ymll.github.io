Countdown timer
  • A timer for couples who are in long distance relationship (LDR) to count their days till reunion.

    I created this countdown timer because I have been in a LDR with my boyfriend for 3 years and I found that it's hard to find a suitable timer for us. I hope the timer can help counting between different timezone and sharing it with 2 persons. The countdown date is currently hard coded and I am changing it to store that dynamically in the cookies so everyone can have their own countdown. One thing I like about the page is the changing background colour according to the time of your region. I'm actually have a lot of enhancement features in my mind. I am planning to write some design docs, but sadly I'm fully packed these days with my day-to-day work. Hopefully I can spare some time on it. If you found this useful as well, or you have any suggestions, please let me know!
